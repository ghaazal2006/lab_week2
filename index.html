Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

Alfredo pasta is a creamy and comforting dish made with pasta, butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese.

It is widely enjoyed because it is simple to prepare and requires only a few basic ingredients that are commonly available in most kitchens. The rich and smooth sauce coats the pasta evenly, giving the dish a mild yet satisfying flavor that appeals to many people.

Chicken Alfredo is a popular variation that includes cooked chicken added to the pasta. The chicken increases the protein content of the meal, making it more filling and nutritious.

This addition also enhances the overall flavor and texture of the dish. As a result, Chicken Alfredo is often served as a complete and hearty meal for lunch or dinner and remains a favorite comfort food for many.

Ingredients

  • 200 g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Cook chicken in a pan until fully cooked.
  3. Melt butter and saut√© garlic.
  4. Add cream and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Mix pasta and chicken with the sauce.
  6. Serve warm.
